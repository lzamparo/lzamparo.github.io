Bio

I'm a researcher who trained in computaional biology, but who has worked other ML domains like NLP. Most recently, I've worked on multimodal models for single-cell and spatial transcriptomics.

I've also done applied ML research in:

  • NLP (semantic parsing, uncertainty modeling)
  • dynamics of functional diversity
  • active learning
  • counterfactual explanation

Before starting my industrial research career, I was a post-doctoral fellow in Christina Leslie's lab at MSKCC, where I worked on models of DNA sequence (using ATAC-seq), chromatin structure (using Hi-C), and sequence embedding. My PhD work involved modeling distributions of phenotypes from high content imaging studies.
